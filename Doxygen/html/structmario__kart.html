Detailed Description

Structure to hold all the variables utilized throughout the game.

Parameters
x- Horizontal coordinate of the car on the track
c- Character of the car itself that shows in the terminal
s- Speed of the car used to adjust speed throughout the track
ts- Time driving without a collision in order to help determine proper speed
st- Start time of the race used to calculate the time the player took to complete the race
ft- Finish time of the race used to calculate the time the player took to complete the race
ss- Speed of the car from 1 to 5 used to adjust speed throughout the track
t- Time it took for the player to complete the race (finish time - start time)
i- Index of cycles completed (distance driven)
id- ID of the game
